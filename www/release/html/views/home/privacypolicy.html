Privacy Policy

Bask is the easy new way to order healthy, delicious food to your doorstep. After learning about your health goals, taste preferences and any special desires or needs, Bask’s intelligent food filtering algorithm gets to work figuring out which menu items are the best match for what you’re craving, day or night.

Each time you order with Bask, you teach us a little more about the types of foods you like and dislike. This allows Bask to further refine what you might be craving – without sacrificing your dietary needs. It’s like having an all-in-one nutritionist, personal chef and delivery service, all at your fingertips.
