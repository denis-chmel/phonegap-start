Get Ready to Bask

Tired of sifting through choices on other sites, searching for something that satisfies your cravings without derailing your diet? Meet Bask: A new meal recommendation service designed to help you make healthier food decisions in a snap.

How it Works

First, we’ll ask you to share a little bit about yourself. Following a vegetarian diet and trying to avoid gluten? Let us know. We’ll deliver personalized meal recommendations tailored to your specific health goals, taste preferences, special dietary needs, and location. Bask draws from a database of over 28,000 restaurants, providing you with variety that will keep your taste buds on edge. With Bask at hand, you’ll always order the perfect meal.

The Special Sauce

From the first time you Bask, our algorithms will start taking note of your goals, preferred diet type, and taste preferences. Let us know what sounds good to you, and we’ll tell you how well-matched each menu option is for your nutritional wants and needs.

Each time you tell Bask what you like, we’ll recommend an increasingly spot-on selection for everything from office lunches to late-night snacks. It’s like having a dietitian, personal chef and delivery service all in one place and at your beck and call.
